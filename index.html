OUR STORY

Growing organic since 1985

For nearly four decades, Belagavi Organics has worked directly with local farmers who share our passion for chemical-free, sustainable agriculture — ensuring every product meets the highest standards of quality, freshness, and nutritional integrity.

Eat clean · Thank a farmer

From our family farms to your family table

Belagavi Organics began as a small collective of farmers in Belagavi district who refused to compromise on the integrity of their land. What started with a single jaggery harvest in 1985 has grown into a network of certified organic farms producing sweeteners, grains, pulses, spices, oils and more — all grown without synthetic pesticides, chemical fertilizers, or preservatives.

Today we work with over fifty local farming families, providing fair prices, traditional seed varieties, and a market that values quality over volume. Every product is harvested at peak ripeness, sun-dried or cold-pressed in small batches, and packed in our IAT Building facility before reaching your home.

When you buy from Belagavi Organics, you are not just buying food — you are supporting a way of life that respects the soil, the farmer, and the eater alike.

OUR PROMISE

Four reasons families trust us

Every product that leaves our facility is backed by these four commitments — non-negotiable principles that have guided our work for forty years.

100% Organic

All our products are certified organic — grown without synthetic pesticides, chemical fertilizers, GMOs, or irradiation. Just clean soil, clean water, and patient farming.

Farm Fresh

Harvested at peak ripeness and processed within days in small batches. No long warehouse storage, no artificial ripening — the way food was meant to be eaten.

Quality Certified

FSSAI licensed and KSDA certified. Each batch is inspected for purity, aroma, and texture before being packed in food-grade, eco-friendly packaging.

Fair to Farmers

We pay our farmers above-market rates and commit to buying their harvest before sowing. Your purchase directly supports fifty+ farming families in Belagavi district.
